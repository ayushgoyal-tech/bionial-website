Capsule Manufacturing

Manufacturing Overview

From HPMC vegetarian capsules to gelatin and softgel formats, our encapsulation line delivers consistent fill weight, dissolution performance, and shelf stability. Available in all standard sizes (0 through 4).

Available Formats & Variants

All variants are manufactured under WHO-GMP conditions with full batch records, in-process checks, and final release testing.

HPMC Capsules

Vegetarian, sizes 0–4. Ideal for herbal extracts, nutraceuticals, probiotics. Available in opaque, transparent, and coloured shells.

Gelatin Capsules

Standard hard-shell gelatin capsules. Cost-effective, suitable for powder and granule fills including minerals and vitamins.

Softgel Capsules

Liquid-fill soft gelatin capsules. Ideal for oil-soluble vitamins (D3, E, K2), Omega-3, CoQ10, and lipid-based APIs.

Modified-Release Capsules

Delayed-release and extended-release fill techniques using enteric-coated granules or matrix pellets inside capsule shells.

Sublingual Capsules

Fast-dissolving, bite-and-swallow format for rapid absorption of B12, melatonin, and other fast-onset nutraceuticals.

How It Works

From inquiry to dispatch in 30 days — our streamlined manufacturing process keeps your project on track at every stage.

01

Consultation & Brief

Share your product requirements — target audience, dosage, packaging preferences, and budget. We'll provide a feasibility assessment and cost estimate within 48 hours.

02

Formulation & Sampling

Our R&D team develops or adapts the formulation. Bench samples are prepared for your approval — colour, texture, dissolution, and taste sign-off.

03

Batch Production

On sample approval, we move to full batch production. Complete batch manufacturing records, in-process checks, and traceability throughout.

04

QC Testing & Dispatch

Final product undergoes complete quality testing before release — assay, dissolution, microbiology, and stability. COA and packaging dispatch within the agreed timeline.
