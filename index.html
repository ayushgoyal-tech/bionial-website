About Bionial

Built for Brands
That Mean Business

Bionial Lifesciences is a full-range nutraceutical contract manufacturer based in Mohali, Punjab — at the heart of North India's pharma manufacturing corridor. Our state-of-the-art facility in the JLPL Industrial Area is equipped to handle everything from R&D formulation to large-scale batch production and dispatch.

Whether you're a D2C startup launching your first SKU or an established brand scaling your product line, we offer the manufacturing flexibility, regulatory expertise, and quality assurance infrastructure to bring your vision to life.
